I'm Sayee Saran, an environmental engineer based in India.

I work as a Senior Consultant at **Advait Energy Transitions Limited**, where I educate emission reduction projects on carbon credits — how they work, how they can help achieve climate goals, and how they contribute to saving the environment.

I believe climate change is the defining challenge of our time, and the most powerful tool we have is education. My work sits at the intersection of carbon markets, sustainability consulting, and climate action.
