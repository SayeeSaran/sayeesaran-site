I've been thinking about building a personal website for a while. Not a polished portfolio or a corporate blog — something more like a garden. A place where ideas can grow, connect, and evolve over time.

## Why a digital garden?

Most blogs are streams — reverse-chronological feeds where the latest post pushes everything else down. A digital garden is different. Notes link to each other. Ideas build on previous ones. Nothing is ever truly "finished" — it just grows.

## What you'll find here

- **Writing** — longer essays on carbon markets, sustainability, and CDR tech
- **Reviews** — notes on books and articles I've read
- **Now** — what I'm currently working on and thinking about

## The tools

This site is built with [Quartz](https://quartz.jzhao.xyz/) and powered by [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/). I write notes locally, and they become web pages. Simple as that.
